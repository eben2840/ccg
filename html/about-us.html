About us

Coastline Citadel Group

Security is now a board-level priority. It is the discipline that protects critical data, safeguards operations, and preserves trust with regulators, customers, and partners. As digital footprints grow, so do the attack surfaces and obligations for control, assurance, and resilience.

Coastline Citadel Group focuses on ISO-aligned and cyber security services: governance and risk, control frameworks, architecture and engineering, security operations, incident response, and cyber resilience. Our teams combine deep regulatory expertise with hands-on technical capability to design, implement, and sustain programmes that stand up to real-world threats and formal audits.
